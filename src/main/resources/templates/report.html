Project Report

Login Page

Sign up will take user to the "Registration Page". Logging in as an ADMIN will take user to the "Admin Dashboard" and logging in as an CUSTOMER will take user to the "Product Catalogue".

Registration Page

User will be redirected back to the "Login Page" once they register.

Admin Dashboard

Admin Dashboard is where the Admin manipulates Products, updates Orders or creating new Products.

Product Catalogue

Customers can browse all available products here. They can click into their "Cart" or "Order History", they can add items in certain quantity to their carts or view the "Product Detail Page" for more details.

Product Detail Page

This page displays more details about the product. Customers can add this product to the cart in certain quantity, click into their "Cart" or go back to "Product Catalogue".

Cart

Customers can view the content of their carts here. The total for each product and for the whole cart is displayed. Users can remove products in their carts or update the quantity for each product. Customers can view their "Order History" or continue shopping in the "Product Catalogue". Customers can also place order which will direct them to their "Order History" afterwards.

Order History

Customers can view their order history here. They can view the products that they ordered and the status of the order. They can also click Continue Shopping to go back to "Product Dialogue"
